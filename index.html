Derek Peruo

“I do classics!”

My focus is classic plays and heightened language like Shakespeare, Molière and Beaumarchais. I think Aquila, The Pearl and New York Classical Theatre do awesome work. I love laughter.

I went to the School of Performing Arts at LaGuardia High School and received my BFA in acting from DePaul University. I also studied Commedia dell’Arte, Feldenkrais, Lecoq, and sword fighting for 5 years.

I played Doofus in David Lindsay-Abaire's off-Broadway premiere of Snow Angel. I also appeared in Much Ado About Nothing, Henry V, Iphigenia In Tauris, Life’s a Dream, and The Mark of Zorro to name a few.
